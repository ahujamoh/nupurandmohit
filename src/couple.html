The Story


Nupur and Mohit were both brought up in different parts of New Delhi. Their paths crossed when they were both looking to get married. From the first introduction to their first meeting, things went like a breeze. Their love blossomed through and through while there were even turbulent times in their lives.




Although both were on different timezones and hated the distance, their was a conviction in them then that the relationship is very important and should be taken to the next step. The families came together and they both were officially roka-fied. This started the wait for them to now be actually married.

You can be now part of their journey in the next big event of their life where they are now getting married and want you to be part of their celebrations. Join us in April 16




In February, 2017, Mohit moved to Boston, putting an end to 5 years of long distance. Now, Sundays mean fresh brewed coffee, decadent homemade breakfasts and Otis Redding on the record player.

Last June, the two took a trip to Portugal, where Mohit proposed on a lookout point over a beach. Nupur was surprised, but not as surprised as when they went to dinner that night and found both their parents and siblings sitting at the restaurant. Suffice it to say, she cried. A LOT.


The two have since built a life together with all the trappings of millennial domesticity - a Slack channel where they decide what to make for dinner on weeknights, a cast iron pan that Mohit lovingly seasons, and a plant family that Nupur named and Mohit nurtures (Rupert, Chaco, Clemence, Esmerelda and Earl).


That's just the first few chapters of the story, folks. Join them in Kalutara for the next!
